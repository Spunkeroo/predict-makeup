Best Drugstore Foundations

L Oreal True Match — A formula so good that professional makeup artists keep it in their kits alongside luxury brands. The extensive shade range with precise undertone options rivals any prestige brand. The natural satin finish, buildable coverage, and all-day wear performance match foundations costing four times as much. Check L Oreal True Match prices on Amazon.

Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless — The best drugstore foundation for oily and combination skin. Micro-powders in the formula blur pores and control shine without a flat, chalky finish. The coverage is buildable from light to medium and the wear time extends well beyond eight hours. At under $9, it competes directly with Estee Lauder Double Wear for a fraction of the cost.

NYX Born to Glow — A radiant, luminous foundation that competes with NARS Sheer Glow at one-third the price. The dewy finish suits dry skin and creates the trendy glazed-skin look. Buildable coverage conceals imperfections without masking skin texture. The 45-shade range demonstrates NYX commitment to inclusivity at the drugstore level.

Best Drugstore Eye Products

Maybelline Lash Sensational Sky High Mascara — The viral mascara that outsells many luxury options for good reason. The flexible wand reaches every lash while the buildable formula adds length and volume without clumping. It holds curl throughout the day and resists smudging. Multiple formulas include original, waterproof, and washable for different needs. One of the best mascaras at any price point.

NYX Ultimate Shadow Palette — Sixteen pigmented, blendable shades for under $18. The formula rivals mid-range palettes like Urban Decay Naked in pigmentation and blend-ability. Available in multiple colorways from warm neutrals to bold brights. The powder is finely milled without being powdery, pressing onto the lid smoothly and blending without muddying colors together.

Essence Lash Princess False Lash Effect Mascara — At under $5, this is the most affordable mascara that genuinely delivers dramatic results. The cone-shaped fiber brush separates and lengthens lashes to an almost false-lash effect. The formula does require careful application to avoid clumps, but the results rival mascaras costing ten times more. A cult favorite with millions of units sold globally.

Best Drugstore Lip Products

Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick — A classic formula that has been perfected over decades. Creamy, pigmented, and comfortable with a massive shade range covering every color family. The moisturizing formula with vitamin E and avocado oil feels luxurious. Shades like Toast of New York, Rum Raisin, and Fire and Ice are legendary. Nothing at the drugstore level matches the shade selection and formula quality.

NYX Lip Lingerie XXL — A liquid lipstick that dries matte without the uncomfortable drying effect of many formulas. Lightweight and transfer-resistant for up to 16 hours. The 24-shade range includes universally flattering nudes, bold reds, and trendy mauves. The precision applicator makes lip lining unnecessary for most wearers.

Maybelline Super Stay Matte Ink — The best long-wear drugstore liquid lipstick. Once set, this formula does not budge through eating, drinking, or mask-wearing. The 40-shade range covers every mood and occasion. Application is smooth and even with a single swipe. The only removal challenge is that it genuinely does not come off without an oil-based cleanser, which is exactly what you want from a long-wear lip product.

Best Drugstore Complexion Products

Maybelline Instant Age Rewind Concealer — The best-selling concealer in America for good reason. The sponge applicator deposits product precisely under the eyes and over blemishes. The formula provides full coverage without creasing, and the cushion applicator blends beautifully. The shade range has expanded to include more than 18 options for a variety of skin tones.

e.l.f. Putty Blush — A cream blush that melts into the skin for a natural flush. The formula is a near-exact dupe for the much more expensive Rare Beauty Soft Pinch Liquid Blush. Tap a small amount onto the apples of your cheeks and blend with your fingertips for a dewy, youthful glow. At $6, buying every shade is cheaper than one luxury blush.

Milani Baked Blush in Luminoso — Widely regarded as the best dupe for NARS Orgasm blush. The warm peach shade with golden shimmer flatters virtually every skin tone. The baked formula is buildable from a subtle glow to a bold flush. This single product has been a makeup artist secret for over a decade and consistently ranks among the best blushes at any price.

Smart Drugstore Makeup Shopping Tips

Check return policies before buying. Most drugstores including CVS, Walgreens, and Target accept returns on opened cosmetics within 30-90 days. This generous policy means you can test foundations, shades, and formulas risk-free. Take advantage of this to experiment with products you might not try otherwise.

BOGO sales and coupon stacking at drugstores can reduce prices by 50% or more. CVS ExtraCare rewards, Walgreens myWalgreens, and Target Circle all offer regular beauty deals. Stock up on repurchases during sales events. Many drugstore brands also run their own sales cycles with predictable timing that savvy shoppers learn to anticipate.

Online reviews and beauty YouTube channels provide extensive drugstore product testing that helps you avoid duds. Channels like Robert Welsh, Theresa Is Dead, and Allie Glines regularly compare drugstore products to their luxury counterparts in detailed side-by-side tests. A few minutes of research before purchasing dramatically improves your hit rate with drugstore makeup purchases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is drugstore makeup safe to use?

Yes, all makeup sold in US drugstores meets FDA safety requirements. Drugstore and luxury brands are held to identical safety standards. Many drugstore products are manufactured in the same facilities as prestige brands. The price difference reflects marketing, packaging, and brand positioning more than ingredient safety.

What is the best overall drugstore makeup brand?

NYX, Maybelline, and e.l.f. consistently produce the best drugstore makeup across categories. NYX offers the best shade ranges and trend-forward products. Maybelline dominates mascara and long-wear formulas. e.l.f. provides the best value with products regularly priced under $8 that compete with mid-range brands.

Are drugstore makeup dupes as good as the originals?

Many drugstore products genuinely match or exceed their high-end counterparts in performance. The gap has narrowed dramatically as drugstore brands invest in better formulations. Some products like Maybelline mascaras and L Oreal foundations are considered equal to luxury equivalents by professional makeup artists.

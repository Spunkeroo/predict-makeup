Best Mascaras 2026: Volume, Length & Waterproof Picks

Chicago demands a lot from your mascara — summer humidity that melts cheap formulas, winter wind that makes your eyes water, and CTA commutes that test smudge-resistance. We tested 15 mascaras through a full Chicago weather cycle to find the ones that actually last.

Best Overall: Maybelline Lash Sensational Sky High ($11)

Flex Tower mascara brush coats every lash from root to tip. Buildable formula goes from natural to dramatic in 2-3 coats. Doesn't flake after 12 hours — tested through a full Chicago workday plus happy hour. Available at every Walgreens, CVS, and Target in the city. At $11, it outperforms mascaras costing 5x more.

Best Waterproof: Lancôme Lash Idôle ($29)

Survived a July afternoon at North Avenue Beach without a single smudge. The bent wand reaches inner and outer corner lashes that other brushes miss. Lifts and separates without clumping. Holds a curl all day even in 90% Chicago humidity. Removes cleanly with oil-based cleanser.

Best Drugstore: L'Oréal Telescopic Lift ($15)

The TikTok-famous mascara lives up to the hype. Ceramic-coated comb separates and lifts every single lash. Creates a false-lash effect without falsies. The comb applicator means zero clumps — every lash is defined. Holds up well in Chicago weather conditions, though not technically waterproof.

Best Luxury: Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Push Up Lashes ($29)

Heart-shaped brush deposits product at the base and tapers toward tips for a natural, lifted look. Contains Collagen Max Complex that conditions lashes over time. The Pillow Talk shade (a slightly tinted black) looks more sophisticated than harsh jet black. Available at Nordstrom on Michigan Ave and Sephora in Lincoln Park.

Best Matte Lipsticks

MAC Retro Matte ($23) — The industry standard for matte lipstick. Ruby Woo (blue-red) is the #1 best-selling lipstick shade worldwide for good reason — it's universally flattering. Intense color payoff in one swipe, no feathering, 8-hour wear time. The formula is drier than modern mattes, so prep with lip balm. 45 matte shades available.

NARS Powermatte Lip Pigment ($28) — Liquid matte that sets to an ultra-thin, weightless finish. One drop delivers opaque, saturated color that survives coffee, meals, and masks. The precision tip applicator gives clean lines without a lip liner. 25 shades from nudes to bold. Cruella (deep red) and American Woman (mauve) are standouts.

Maybelline SuperStay Matte Ink ($10) — Best drugstore matte. Genuinely lasts 16 hours without touch-ups. The arrow applicator makes precise application easy. Lover (warm pink) and Pioneer (deep berry) are cult favorites. Only downside: requires oil-based remover — makeup wipes won't cut it. At $10, it outperforms most $30 lipsticks.

Best Satin Lipsticks

Charlotte Tilbury Matte Revolution ($37) — Despite the name, this is a comfortable satin-matte with a slight sheen. Pillow Talk (nude pink) is the brand's hero shade and genuinely flatters every skin tone — it's the shade equivalent of a magic trick. Enriched with orchid extract for lip care benefits. 5-hour wear, reapplies beautifully.

YSL Rouge Pur Couture ($42) — Luxury satin lipstick with creamy texture and buildable coverage. The sleek gold case is iconic. Le Rouge (classic red) has been a best-seller for decades. Infused with jojoba and macadamia oils for comfortable all-day wear. 30 shades.

Revlon Super Lustrous ($9) — Best budget satin lipstick. This 80-year-old formula has been continuously improved and remains excellent. Micro-fine color pigments in a moisturizing base. 82 shades — the largest range at any price point. Toast of New York (warm nude) and Cherries in the Snow (blue-red) are iconic.

Best Lip Glosses

Fenty Beauty Gloss Bomb ($22) — The gloss that reignited the lip gloss trend. Fenty Glow (shimmering rose) is universally flattering and the most Instagrammed lip product since 2017. Non-sticky formula with shea butter for moisture. Subtle color payoff with high shine. Perfect alone or over lipstick.

NYX Butter Gloss ($6) — Cult-favorite drugstore gloss. Creamy, buttery texture with zero stickiness. Creme Brulee (warm nude) sells out repeatedly. 34 shades including clear. At $6, buy several to keep in your bag, car, desk, and bedside table.

Application Tips

  1. Exfoliate first: Use a lip scrub or soft toothbrush to remove flakes. Lipstick looks terrible on chapped lips
  2. Moisturize, then blot: Apply lip balm 10 minutes before lipstick. Blot excess with a tissue — you want hydrated lips, not a slippery surface
  3. Line your lips: Use a matching lip liner to define your lip shape and prevent feathering. Fill in the entire lip with liner as a base for longer wear
  4. Apply lipstick from the center: Start at the cupid's bow and work outward. Use the bullet's shape to follow your natural lip line
  5. Blot and reapply: For maximum longevity, apply one coat, blot with a tissue, dust translucent powder over the tissue on your lips, then apply a second coat
  6. Clean up edges: Use a small concealer brush with concealer to sharpen your lip line and correct any mistakes

Frequently Asked Questions

What lipstick shade suits everyone?

MAC Velvet Teddy (warm nude), Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk (nude pink), and Clinique Black Honey (sheer berry) are considered the three most universally flattering shades across all skin tones.

How do I make lipstick last all day?

Exfoliate, apply lip liner as a base, apply lipstick, blot with tissue, dust translucent powder over the tissue, then apply a second coat. This technique doubles wear time for any formula.

Are expensive lipsticks worth it?

For everyday wear, drugstore options like Maybelline SuperStay perform excellently. Luxury lipsticks offer better comfort, packaging, and shade curation. Both have a place in a well-rounded collection.

What is the difference between matte and satin lipstick?

Matte has zero shine and maximum color payoff but can feel drying. Satin has a slight sheen, feels more comfortable, and is more forgiving on dry lips. Satin is better for everyday wear; matte for long-lasting bold looks.

How often should I replace lipstick?

Lipsticks expire after 12-18 months once opened. Signs of expiration include changes in smell, texture, or color. Liquid lipsticks tend to expire faster than bullet lipsticks.
